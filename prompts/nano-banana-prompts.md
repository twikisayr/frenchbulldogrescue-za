# Nano Banana Prompt Library (FBR SA)

## Global style rules
- Photoreal, warm, trustworthy, compassionate
- South African context where possible
- Natural light, clean backgrounds, no clutter
- No text overlay, no logos, no watermarks
- No graphic distress, no cages, no fear visuals

## Negative prompt block
"text overlay, watermark, logo, lowres, blurry, cartoon, harsh flash, graphic injury, dirty kennel, aggressive posture, cluttered background"

## 1) Homepage hero (`hero-frenchie-surrender`)
Prompt:
"Photoreal French Bulldog with caring owner in a calm South African home environment, warm natural lighting, clean composition, hopeful rescue tone, high detail, editorial photography"

## 2) Surrender process image (`section-surrender-process`)
Prompt:
"Photoreal rescue coordinator gently handling a French Bulldog indoors, compassionate and professional atmosphere, neutral modern interior, natural light, trust-building tone"

## 3) Health guide image (`section-frenchie-care`)
Prompt:
"Photoreal French Bulldog at a vet wellness check in South Africa, calm dog, professional vet context, clean clinical setting, soft natural light, informative trustworthy style"

## 4) Trust network image (`section-rescue-network`)
Prompt:
"Photoreal collage-style scene of responsible rescue teamwork in South Africa, French Bulldog focus, positive and professional mood, clean modern visual style"

## 5) Open Graph image (`og-frenchie-surrender`)
Prompt:
"Photoreal close-up French Bulldog portrait with soft neutral background, high contrast subject, social-share friendly framing, premium rescue brand tone"

## Output specs
- Hero/section: 1600x1000 preferred
- OG: 1200x630 exact
- Keep source filenames descriptive before processing
