KZN-specific practical reality

Heat and humidity can increase risk for French Bulldogs with breathing issues. If your Frenchie is already struggling, urgent veterinary support may need to happen before rescue placement planning.

What to include in your request

Say where you are in KwaZulu-Natal, whether the case is urgent today, and whether there are any known breathing, allergy, skin, or mobility issues. That helps the rescue team assess risk quickly.
