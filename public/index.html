Our Commitment to You & Your Dog

Operating across Gauteng, Cape Town, and KwaZulu-Natal, providing remote support wherever possible.

Confidential Support

We handle surrender requests discreetly. Your details are protected and only shared with the dedicated team handling your specific case.

Home-Based Fosters

We do not use a public classifieds handover model or stressful kennels. Where possible, dogs are placed directly into stable, vetted foster homes to reduce stress.

Breed-Aware Care

Frenchies have highly specific medical and heat-related risks. Our transport and care process accounts for those breed realities from day one.

Common Questions

Will my details stay private?

Yes. Surrender requests are handled with confidentiality and only shared with the team coordinating your case.

Do you judge owners who surrender?

No. We focus entirely on your dog’s safety and practical next steps.

What if my dog has severe medical needs?

Please include full, honest details in your form so we can plan safer placement support and veterinary steps.
