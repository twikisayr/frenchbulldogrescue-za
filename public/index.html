Need to surrender a French Bulldog in South Africa?

You’re not alone, and you’re not being judged. If you need help now, we’ll guide you through a safe, confidential process that puts your dog’s welfare first.

Surrender a Frenchie What happens next
Coverage: Gauteng, Cape Town, and KwaZulu-Natal, with remote support where possible.

How we help

Confidential support

We handle surrender requests discreetly. Your details are protected and only shared with the team handling your case.

Home-based foster network

No kennel handover model. Where possible, dogs are placed in stable foster homes to reduce stress.

Breed-aware care

Frenchies have specific medical and heat-related risks. Our process accounts for those realities from day one.
