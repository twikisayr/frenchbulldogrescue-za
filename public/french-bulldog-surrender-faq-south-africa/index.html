French Bulldog surrender FAQ, South Africa

If you are overwhelmed, these are the most common questions people ask before submitting a surrender request.

Start confidential form Read surrender guide

Will my details stay private?

Yes. Your details are handled confidentially and are only used to coordinate safe next steps for your Frenchie.

Will I be judged for asking for help?

No. The focus is your dog’s safety and a practical path forward, not blame.

What if my French Bulldog has medical issues?

Include full honest details, especially breathing, heat, skin, spine, allergy, or mobility concerns. That helps triage happen more safely.

How quickly will someone reply?

Urgent cases are prioritised same day where possible. Non-urgent requests are usually answered within 24 hours.

Can I submit the form if I do not have every detail yet?

Yes. Start with the core details first, then add more information later if needed.

What happens after I submit?

The team reviews urgency, medical needs, and location, then contacts you with the safest next steps.
