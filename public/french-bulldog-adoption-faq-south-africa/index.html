French Bulldog adoption FAQ, South Africa

These are the most common questions people ask before committing to adopt a Frenchie.

Adoption guide Readiness checklist

Are French Bulldogs expensive to care for?

They can be. Ongoing veterinary care, heat management, allergies, skin issues, and possible breathing problems all add up.

Is a French Bulldog a good first dog?

Possibly, but only if you are realistic about the breed’s needs and do not assume they are easy just because they are small.

Why does rescue adoption take time?

Because good rescue matching is careful. The aim is a stable placement that works for the dog long term.

Should I rescue instead of buying?

If your goal is an ethical, welfare-first placement, rescue-first is the stronger path.

What should I do before applying?

Check your household readiness honestly, understand likely costs, and read how the rescue process works before you get emotionally committed.
