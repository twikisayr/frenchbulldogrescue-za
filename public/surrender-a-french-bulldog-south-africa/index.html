Quick surrender FAQs

How quickly will someone contact me?
Urgent requests are prioritised for same-day response where possible. Non-urgent requests are usually answered within 24 hours.

Will my personal details be shared publicly?
No. Your details are handled confidentially and used only to coordinate safe next steps for your Frenchie.

Can I submit if I do not have every detail yet?
Yes. Submit core details first so we can triage quickly, then add extra information later.

What details help the rescue team assess my Frenchie faster?
Age, sex, sterilisation status, medical issues, behaviour around children/dogs/cats, your province, and how urgent the handover is are the most useful starting details.
