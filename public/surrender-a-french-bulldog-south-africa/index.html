Surrender a French Bulldog (Confidential)

If you can’t keep your Frenchie, we’ll help you do this safely and responsibly. Submit the form below and we’ll contact you with next steps.

This is a judgement-free process. Our first priority is your dog’s safety.

What happens next

1) We review your request

We check urgency, medical needs, and location details.

2) We contact you

We confirm practical handover options and immediate care requirements.

3) Safe placement process

We move toward foster placement or a managed transfer pathway.

Start your surrender request

Confidential enquiry form. If your case is urgent, please select “Yes - urgent today” and include immediate safety concerns in your notes.

By submitting, you confirm the information is accurate and that we may contact you to coordinate safe next steps.
